Mona Khan Company

Dance Instructor

As a dance instructor, I have learned how to communicate with students, work with other people who share the same passion as me, and collaborate to ensure that everyone who enters my class has fun.

HC Bollywood

Airband Leader

Ever since freshman year, I have been a co-leader of Bollywood. Organizing a group of 50 people has not been easy, but it has been worth it. Ordering costumes, choreographing dances, figuring out formations, and more taught me harmony in a team. In the end, with good leadership and a good attitude, anything can be accomplished.

Additional Airbands

Airband Leader (HC/MC)

Along with HC Bollywood, I have also led HC Fight Set, HC Ballroom, and MC Raas. Every year, I have also choreographed and taught the unity dance. Time management is a big asset of being responsible for so many different dances, but keeping a positive outlook is what motivates me to be a better leader.

ICC Summer Camps

Camp Counselor

Every summer since freshman year, I have volunteered at ICC as a counselor. In my second year I was a senior counselor, and last year I became a head counselor. This responsibility meant that I was in chargge of campers as well as leading the other junior counselors to having a successful summer.
